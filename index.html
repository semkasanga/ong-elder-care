Who We Are

Champions for Zambia's Most Vulnerable Elders

AGED-CARE FOUNDATION is a registered non-profit and charitable organization dedicated to promoting the welfare, dignity, health, protection, and social inclusion of older persons in Zambia. The Foundation was established to address the growing challenges faced by vulnerable elderly persons through sustainable and evidence-based interventions.

We work with communities, healthcare institutions, correctional facilities, government agencies, development partners, faith-based organizations, and the private sector to improve the quality of life of older persons across the nation.

  • Poverty
  • Neglect & abandonment
  • Poor health
  • Social isolation
  • Abuse
  • Inadequate access to support services
Older persons are custodians of knowledge, culture, and community values. We strive to ensure every older person enjoys a life of dignity, respect, security, inclusion, and wellbeing.

Our Purpose

Mission, Vision & Values

AGED-CARE FOUNDATION is committed to advancing the welfare, dignity, health, protection, and social inclusion of vulnerable and neglected older persons across Zambia through compassionate service delivery, advocacy, research, strategic partnerships, and community-driven interventions.

Our Mission

To improve the quality of life of vulnerable older persons through care services, health interventions, advocacy, research, empowerment programmes, and strategic partnerships.

Our Vision

A Zambia where every older person lives with dignity, good health, protection, and social inclusion.

Our Strategic Goal

To become Zambia's leading organization promoting the welfare, protection, health, and social inclusion of vulnerable older persons by 2030.

Our Core Values

Dignity

Every older person deserves a life of dignity, respect, and security.

Health

Promoting good health, wellbeing, and active ageing for all older persons.

Protection

Safeguarding vulnerable elders from neglect, abuse, and social harm.

Social Inclusion

Ensuring no elderly person is isolated, forgotten, or left behind.

Compassionate Service

Delivering practical, human-centered care with empathy and presence.

Accountability

Strong governance, financial stewardship, and evidence-based programming.

Our Strategic Pillars

Seven Pillars of Compassionate Action

Through evidence-based interventions and community-driven programmes, we address the full spectrum of needs faced by vulnerable older persons across Zambia.

Elderly Welfare & Livelihood Support

Practical assistance to improve the quality of life of vulnerable older persons.

  • Food and nutritional support
  • Clothing and blanket distribution
  • Personal hygiene and dignity kits
  • Emergency relief support
  • Home visitation programmes
  • Community support networks

Health & Wellbeing

Promoting healthy and active ageing across communities.

  • Health screening and outreach programmes
  • Mental health and psychosocial support
  • Health education and awareness campaigns
  • Caregiver support and training
  • Medical referrals and follow-up services
  • Wellness and active ageing initiatives

Advocacy, Rights & Social Inclusion

Championing the rights of older persons and building age-friendly communities.

  • Public awareness campaigns
  • Elder abuse prevention programmes
  • Policy advocacy and engagement
  • Community dialogues
  • Intergenerational engagement initiatives

Hospital & Correctional Facility Support
